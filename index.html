Fun Challenges to Try in Mines

Goal: Uncover as many safe tiles as possible in 60 seconds.

How to Play: Set a timer and play with a low mine count (e.g., 2 mines on a 5x5 grid). Focus on quick decisions rather than overthinking. Cash out before the timer runs out to secure your multiplier. This challenge tests your speed and gut instinct.

Tip: Practice in demo mode on Teen Patti Stars to hone your reflexes.

Goal: Play with a high mine count (e.g., 10 mines on a 5x5 grid) and aim for a 3x multiplier.

How to Play: Increase the mine count for a tougher grid. Your goal is to reach a 3x multiplier without hitting a bomb. This challenge pushes your risk tolerance and strategic planning.

Tip: Start with edge tiles to maximize safe picks early on.

Goal: Win with only 3 tile picks.

How to Play: Set a moderate mine count (e.g., 5 mines) and limit yourself to uncovering just three tiles before cashing out. This forces you to think carefully about each move.

Goal: Uncover tiles in a specific pattern (e.g., a cross or diagonal).

How to Play: Choose a pattern before starting and stick to it, regardless of the grid. For example, only pick tiles that form a diagonal line. This adds a creative twist and tests your discipline.

Tip: Start with a low mine count to make this challenge more achievable.

Goal: Play 10 consecutive games without hitting a bomb.
